About Us

Welcome to our car loan service. Here is the process for getting a car loan:

  1. Submit an enquiry: Fill out the car loan enquiry form on our website with your basic details and the amount you wish to borrow.
  2. Review and contact: Our team will review your enquiry and contact you within 24 hours to gather more details and answer any questions you may have.
  3. Application processing: We will process your application by verifying your provided information and assessing your creditworthiness.
  4. Approval and terms: Upon approval, we will send you the loan offer detailing the loan amount, interest rate, repayment schedule, and other terms.
  5. Signing and disbursement: After you sign the loan agreement, we will disburse the loan amount to your account, enabling you to purchase your car.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a car loan, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 21 years old.
  • Have a steady source of income.
  • Have a good credit history.

Required Documents

When applying for a car loan, please have the following documents ready:

  • Proof of identity (e.g., passport, driver's license).
  • Proof of income (e.g., salary slips, bank statements).
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bills).
